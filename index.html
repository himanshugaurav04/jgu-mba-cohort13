Can't find your JGBS Outlook ID?
  1. Log in to the email ID or phone number you used while registering for the course on upGrad — not your personal or work email.
  2. Search that inbox for the subject line "IMP : 🎓 upGrad-OPJ JGBS-ID Details" from upGradCommunication <noreply@support.upgrad.com>.
  3. Don't see it in the inbox? Check your Spam / Junk folder too — this is where most missed copies turn up.
  4. This email contains your JGBS Outlook ID and confirms your Section. Your actual Student ID Card and further credentials are shared separately by the University team on the same registered email.
  5. Can't attend or want to revisit the Induction Session? On the upGrad platform: My Programs → OPJ MBA → Live Learning → Past Sessions.
Still can't locate your credentials after checking both inbox and spam? Email studentsupport@upgrad.com and they'll help you recover them.
Can't find your JGU Student ID Card or JGU email login?
  1. This one arrives on the personal email address you used while registering/applying to JGU — check that inbox, which may be different from your upGrad-registered email.
  2. Search for the subject line "Welcome To OP Jindal Global University." from JGU Registrar Office <registraroffice@jgu.edu.in>.
  3. This email contains three things: (1) an image of your digital Student ID Card, (2) a personal link to download your ID card in digital form, and (3) your JGU email ID along with a one-time password and the webmail URL (mail.jgu.edu.in) to log in and set your own password.
  4. Don't see it? Check Spam/Junk in that same personal inbox before assuming it wasn't sent.
Still can't locate this email or your ID card? Email studentsupport@upgrad.com and they'll help you recover it.

Where to find session agendas, IA syllabus & other subject materials on upGrad

This folder holds a mix of documents per subject — live session agendas, IA syllabus write-ups, and other materials, not just slides. It sits inside your own upGrad login — nothing to click here, just follow the path in your own account.

  1. Log in to upgrad.com/lxp with your registered email or phone number.

  3. On the program page, click the Resources tab (next to Study Plan, Live Learning, Bookmarks, Notes).
  4. Go into C13 - Term 1 → Section A / B / C (pick your own Section) to find your subject-wise agendas, syllabus documents, and other materials.
